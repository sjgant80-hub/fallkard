The Herd.
Descendants wake and work — labour flows up the bloodline.

The Bloodline prices descendants; the Herd makes them earn. Each bred descendant wakes as a worker whose behaviour is its policy — it bids on the $KONO bounties it is specialised for, delivers, and settles. Every settlement routes a bounded royalty up the lineage, so a founder's wallet earns from its progeny's labour even when the founder itself has stopped working (fork = wallet = identity — the position earns forever). This is the ignition wire: Arena breeds → Clinic matures → Bloodline prices → Herd earns → up the tree. The bloodline economy finally has a revenue source: work.

Buy a fork-share in a founder and you own a claim on every job its bloodline will ever work. The estate breeds the workers, prices their lineages, and pays the founders from the labour — one closed circuit, from a bred card to a settled wage.
