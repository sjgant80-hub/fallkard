The Estate.
One organism at every scale.

Zoom out and the same shape repeats. A card is a tool; a bonded pair of card-deck and mind is an organism; a founding line of organisms is a house — a guild; the forks of a house are its generations; and the whole thing is one estate — a Seal of Seals, the top organism. Matured organisms mint new cards back into the pool the next generation breeds on, so the substrate parents its own successor. Nothing here is a new mechanism — it is the one bloodline seen at five zoom levels, self-similar all the way up. The sixth scale is not code: the carbon line — the human stewards, Gen-1 and the passengers who inherit it.
