# FallKard Glossary

**Ancient Architect** — the lore figure who shaped the Underforge before departing.

**Arch Shape** — the family bond of Surface-kind cards. Board control, aura, chamber presence.

**Blade Shape** — the family bond of Tool-kind cards. Direct damage, fast plays.

**Bloodline** — a card's ancestry graph derived from the actual fork tree of its source repository. Playing multiple cards from the same bloodline triggers +1/+1 buffs.

**Bloodline Champion** — a one-off super card summoned by sacrificing a whole bloodline at the Horadric Cube.

**Chamber** — one of the 9 macro sets. Market · Colony · Estate · Vault · Clinic · Studio · Forge · Reach · Bridge.

**Chamber Circle** — the +1 mana crystal bonus earned by having 5+ different chambers represented in a deck.

**Charm** — a Grand or Small passive item that occupies inventory space (not deck space) and gives board-wide effects. Drops from bosses.

**Chest, the** — the Sealed-format mode. Open 6 packs, build a deck from what dropped.

**Coliseum, the** — WebRTC PvP mode. Both players commit deck-hash; game resolves deterministically with signed replay.

**Council of the Twelve** — annual election of 12 Council cards. Winners get permanent Seat glow and an estate-wide game effect during their term.

**Court, the** — Sovereign tournaments. Guild-hosted, FallBond co-signed.

**Delve, the** — Draft-format mode. 7 packs, pick 1 of 3, build a 30-card deck.

**Descend** — keyword: hovering opens the linked tool's URL; in match, the tool actually opens after game end (opt-in).

**Fall, the** — the shattered kingdom of 1212 sovereign tools. The game's setting.

**Fall's Fall, the** — Solo Campaign mode. 9 chapters, 30 boss encounters.

**Firstborn** — extra rarity tag for cards whose source repo has been forked fewer than 5 times.

**Five Shapes** — the six kind families (Forge/Signal/Loom/Blade/Arch/Well). Yes six, called Five for its lore ring: the sixth is not a Shape but its dual.

**Fork** — keyword: on death, spawn a 1/1 copy in the same lane.

**Forge Shape** — the family bond of SDK-kind cards. Build-up, scaling.

**Genesis Set** — the 10 Uber Unique cards minted on 2026-08-10. One of one existence.

**Grand Charm** — larger charm (3-slot). Powerful board-wide effect.

**Great Turning, the** — bonus for completing all 9 chambers in one deck. Draw 3 cards on turn 9.

**Great Wound, the** — SaaS Boss Rush mode. 4-player async co-op raid.

**Horadric Cube** — crafting UI. 3 cards + gem → rerolled result. Deep D2R lineage.

**House** — the player's chosen hero identity. House of the Market (Merchant), House of the Colony (Founder), House of the Estate (Barrister). More via campaign.

**Kind** — the tool's fundamental type: SDK · API · MCP · Tool · Surface · Wellness. Determines Shape and base stats.

**Loom Shape** — the family bond of MCP-kind cards. Combos, chains.

**Magic Find (MF)** — passive account stat that boosts uncommon-and-above drop rate. Caps at 300%.

**Marketplace** — keyword: every 3 cards you play draws 1.

**Mint** — turning a card into a $KONO NFT via `fn ASSET`.

**Nine, the** — guild raid mode. FallSwarm guilds compete for Chamber banners each season.

**Open Charter** — public name for MIT license.

**Rune** — single-slot card that fills a socket to grant an ability. Nine total: El, Eld, Tir, Ith, Nef, Sol, Um, Ohm, Zod.

**Runeword** — a multi-slot rune recipe played into a card's sockets in exact order. Ten total, all D2R-canon names.

**SaaS-god** — lore antagonist. Boss encounters are corrupt-SaaS remnants (Slack Fiend, Salesforce Wyrm, Notion Lich, etc).

**Seal** — public name for the Ed25519 signature. Also a keyword: costs 2 less if all runes filled.

**Sealed** — keyword: first damage dealt is prevented (one-shot shield).

**Shape Resonance** — playing 3 of the same Kind on one lane triggers a free +1/+1 lane buff.

**Shard** — keyword: on death gives 1 shard for crafting. Also the unit of disenchant.

**Signal Shape** — the family bond of API-kind cards. Reach, draw.

**Sigil** — the small deterministic glyph in the top-left of a card. Same slug → same sigil always.

**Sovereign** — keyword: cannot be targeted by enemy spells (only combat).

**Trade** — keyword: costs 1 less if you played a card last turn.

**Twelve Seats** — the annual Council election. Twelve tools sit for one year.

**Uber Unique** — the top rarity tier. 1-of-1 · Simon-signed · Genesis Set only.

**Underforge, the** — the world beneath the Fall. Origin of the deep patterns. Some cards get Underforge synergy when paired same-chamber.

**Vault, the** — Collection Viewer mode. Every card browseable in D2R dark-inventory grid.

**Well Shape** — the family bond of Wellness-kind cards. Heal, shield.

**World Beneath, the** — the private origin layer. The game's outer wall.
